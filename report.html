REPORT WRITING

An incident which is publish in the newspaper is called Report.

1. At first, we have to write the Heading in capital form.

Example:

“A BUS ACCIDENT, 3 DIED”

2. Secondly, below the heading, reporter name has to be added.

Example:

By a staff reporter/ By your name

3. Next, from the left corner, we have to mention Place name and Date.

Example:

“Kolkata, 20th September”

4. Next, we have to write details about the incident like: what incident was happened in where, etc. in Past tense.

5. We have to point out details about the victims, etc. point wise in Past tense.

6. We also have to mention the role of Police, Politicians, etc. in Past tense.

7. At last, we have to finish the report by writing some sentences.

As like :

The Police have arrested the driver of the bus. (for Road accident report)

Investigation is going on. (for robbery report)

Chief Minister/ State Government has reviewed the situation and assured of all help. (for natural disaster)
