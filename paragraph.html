PARAGRAPH

PARAGRAPH WRITING

1. First, we have to write a Title/Heading in Capital.

Example:

THE CROW AND THE FOX (character wise name) Grasp all, lose all (Moral wise name)

2. Secondly, we have to make sentences according to the point.

3. Maximum 15 to 20 sentences have to write.

4. We have to write sentences according to the Tense on the basis of Topic.

Example:

A MEMORABLE DAY/ A TRAIN JOURNEY/ YOUR FIRST DAY AT SCHOOL (use Past form for this type of topic)

YOUR FAVOURITE AUTHOR/PARENTS/TEACHER/BOOK/ ABOUT YOURSELF/ FRIENDSHIP/ DISCIPLINE/ MANNERS, etc. (use Present form for this type of topic)

YOUR AIM IN LIFE/ YOUR PLAN AFTER EXAMINATION, etc. (use Present and Future form for this type of topic)

CURRENT AFFAIRS/ GLOBAL WARMING/ CHILD LABOUR/ MOBILE PHONE/ SAFE DRIVE, etc. (use Present and Past and Future according to the point)
