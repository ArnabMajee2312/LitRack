SUMMERY WRITING

Summary means to write the main words from the passage. In that case, if someone writes more, he will get less marks instead of getting more marks. May not even get marks. So write by knowing the correct rules of writing.

1. Can't write more sentences.

2. No numbering can be done.

3. It should be written in one paragraph.

4. 1 of 3 parts of the passage should be written.

5. No question mark, example and quote can be written or written.

6. After understanding the entire passage, you have to start writing.

7. You have to try to understand whether the passage is written about something bad or something good.

8. You need to know what the passage is about.

9. Must write in own language.

10. In no way can the Passage be copied exactly.

11. No title or heading can be done.

12. If the main passage is 90 words, then summary should be written between 25 - 35 words.
