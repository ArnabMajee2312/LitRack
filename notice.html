NOTICE WRITING

1. At first for Notice writing, we have to start by this line:

“It’s hereby informed/ notified that ………… (Notice subject)” (for Club purposes)

OR

“The students are hereby informed/members of our school/club are informed that ………. (Notice subject)” (for School/College purposes)

2. Then we have to write the body of the subject in short form.

3. At the end, we have to write:

“For further/more information/details contact the undersigned person/ One may contact the undersigned for mor/further details”

OR

“Co-operation for all is highly solicited”
