STORY WRITING

1. First, we have to write the story name in capital form. (Mainly, the main character of the story or the moral of the story is written as Title)

Example:

THE CROW AND THE FOX (character wise name) Grasp all, lose all (Moral wise name)

2. Secondly, we have to start writing by this format:

3. Once/Once upon a time/ One day – subject – 2nd form of Verb - object

4. Before writing, we have to read all the related point to write a story.

5. Then, we have to make sentence serially according to the point in Past Tense.

6. We have to add many extra sentences to connect one point to another to maintain succession.

7. We have to remember that while writing the story, the points shouldn’t be in disorder.

8. We have to write one or two sentences in direct speech.

Example:

The Crow said to the Fox, “I know what you want.”

9. At last, we must have to write a Moral sentence.

Example:

“Tit for Tat”/ “Look before you Leap”, etc.
