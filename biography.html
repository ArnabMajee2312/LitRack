BIOGRAPHY & AUTOBIOGRAPGY WRITING

If we write about any famous person is called BIOGRAPHY WRITING.

Mainly it’s written by 3rd person (He, She).

If any writer writes about her/his own life is called AUTOBIOGRAPHY WRITING.

It’s written by 1st person (I, We).

1. First, we have to write the name in capital form.

Example:

ANNIE BESANT

2. Secondly, we have to write about the person, why he/she is/was famous, his/her quotes by some sentences.

Example:

Annie Besant was/is a famous British writer and social artist.

3. Thirdly, we have to write about her/his birth date, year and place.

Example:

He/ She was born on 1st October, 1847 in London.

4. Fourthly, we have to write about her/his parents name, about her/his childhood, education life and many more points in Past (Past Tense) form.

5. Next, we have to write about her/his work/service life, innovations, her/his writings, achievement in Past (Past Tense) form.

6. Next, we have to point out about her/his prizes, medals, post, designation, etc.

7. Next, we have to write about her/his dead life.

Example:

This great person/he/she died/passed away/left/took his/her last breath on date in year. She (Annie Besant) took her last breath on 20th September on 1933 at the age of 85.

8.Lastly, we have to finish by this line “She/He is no more with us, but her/his contributions will last forever”

*If He/ She is still alive, we have to finish by this line

“She/He is still working hard to perform better in the fourth coming events/ Olympics/ Asiad” (for Player Persons)

“She/He is still working for the betterment of our society or children” (for Social Workers)
