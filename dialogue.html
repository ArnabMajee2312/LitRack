DIALOGUE WRITING

The conversation between two or more people is called Dialogue.

1. At first, we have to start conversation by saying “Hi/ Hello!”

2. We have to talk some other subject then move to the main topic.

3. We have to use short/contracted form in the conversation.

Example:

I am = I’m, I have = I’ve, He cannot = He can’t, etc.

4. We have to finish conversation by saying “I’ve to go now. See you later. Nice to meet you. Good Bye”
